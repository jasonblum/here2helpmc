Here2Help is a grassroots, all-volunteer organization that combats local food insecurity by providing free grocery essentials to the area's most vulnerable residents.

Headquartered in Chevy Chase, MD, Here2Help makes safe, contact-free deliveries to Montgomery County, MD, residents who request them.

Please be aware that we mostly distribute non-perishable items such as rice and pasta; canned vegetables and fruit; canned tuna or chicken; canned or dried beans; peanut butter; and breakfast cereal. In addition, a limited amount of feminine hygiene, toilet paper, and other non-food essentials may also be requested and are included if supplies are available.

Please also be aware that you must submit a request for each week you want a delivery.

For more information about Here2Help, please visit www.Here2HelpMC.com.
